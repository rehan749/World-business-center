WORLD BUSINESS CENTRE GREATER NOIDA WEST

Centrally positioned in Greater Noida West, World Business Centre is an innovative kind of commercial project that has been envisioned by a real estate company, World Infracons. World Business Centre is a commercial project in Noida that has been designed with utmost sincerity and in-depth research understanding the market demand. The builder group has smartly chosen the project site location in Noida Extension as it is a developing city with huge untapped market for commercial developments.
Located in Knowledge Park V, Noida Extension, World Business Centre commercial project offers IT/ITES Office Space, Retail, Shopping and Entertainment, Space for Food Court, Corporate Suites & Bank / ATM Space. World Business Center Noida Extension is well connected with NH24, FNG Expressway and DND Flyway. This commercial project is a hypermarket with a wide variety of retail brand options to browse through. Designed to house national and foreign retail brands of all categories. World Business Centre retail shops are ready to offer all kinds of retail solutions to the consumers to set up any kind of retail stores like vanilla store, kiosk, anchor store, hyper store, branded retail store, showroom and more. The extensive food court created on top of the retail floors present a wide plethora of multi-sized restaurant spaces to set up multi-cuisine food joints of all varieties starting from a small take away kiosk to an extensive dining area.

World Business Centre Greater Noida West located in a plush location with a captive catchment area inhabited in existing residential properties and more to arrive in near future, This Business Center is assured to enjoy huge footfall while giving a boost to the retail businesses parking at this retail arcade. A huge parking area has been designed in this shopping mall to provide hassle free parking service for the investors and visitors.
